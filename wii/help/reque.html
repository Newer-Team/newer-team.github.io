To play Newer, you'll need a few things:

Wii or Wii U Console (EU/US/JPN).
The Wii Mini is not supported.
New Super Mario Bros. Wii game disc
(European/Australian, Japanese or US version)
SD or SDHC card (if you'll be playing on a Wii) or SD card (if you'll be playing on a Wii U) with at least 570 MB of free space, formatted to FAT.
A way to access SD/SDHC cards from your computer.
I've got these!

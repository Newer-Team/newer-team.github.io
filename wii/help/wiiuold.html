Newer for Wii U

Riivolution (and thus Newer) will now function on Wii U, in virtual Wii mode. Simply download riivolution as normal, but instead of using letterbomb (as described in the instructions), use either the homebrew channel, or a game based exploit for: LEGO Indiana Jones, Super Smash Bros. Brawl, Yu-Gi-Oh 5D's Wheelie Breakers, LEGO Batman, Lego Star Wars, or Tales of Symphonia: Dawn of the New World.

Follow the instructions on the links above as if they were letterbomb. More detailed instructions on the help page will follow when we've had the chance to write them out.

Thanks and best of luck. - the Newer Team.

Newer Worlds

Click on a world to explore the map.

World 1: Yoshi's Island

The beginnings of your Newer adventure take you through a ruined temple, a swamp, various forests and more...! With your friendly dinosaur companion at hand, of course.

World 2: Rubble Ruins

After a trek through the Mushroom Kingdom's finest sewer system, you'll arrive bang in the middle of the sweltering desert. Take shelter from Angry Sun and hope for the best!

World A: Goldwood Forest

The path to this secret world is hidden somewhere among Yoshi's Island... can you find it? There's five secret worlds in all for you to uncover!

Yoshi's House

World 1-1: Palm Beach
The start of Mario's adventure. A hilly but peaceful beach with lots of sunshine.
 
World 1-3: Springwater Swamp
A deep, dark and flooded forest with lots of life in its waters. Watch out for Wigglers!
 
World 1's Tree Tower
A tall tower disguised as a tree. King Fuzzy stands guard at the very top, protected by an army of walking and climbing Koopa Troopas.
 
World 1-6: Rainshed Pond
A giant pool of rainwater. Rumours say there's a big secret in it somewhere - can you find it?
 
World 1-8: Switch Shift Grove
A switch-filled level where anything and everything can move. Keep an eye out, it'd be easy to hide something here...
 
Shop
Your friendly neighbourhood store where you can exchange spare Star Coins for valuable powerups.
 
Challenge House
A place to test your skills. Come and try your hand at winning some free powerups!

Anything that Mario still remembers about plumbing will be put to the test in this wet, pipe-laden area!
 
World 2-8: Searing Sands
Watch out! Angry Sun is lurking around this level, following Mario's every move and ready to make his day a bit hotter...

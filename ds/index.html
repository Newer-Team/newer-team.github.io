Newer DS is a mod of New Super Mario Bros. that sets Mario out on an all-new adventure!
It's a sequel to our own Newer Super Mario Bros. Wii.

It's completely free, so what are you waiting for?

Download Newer DS!

Current release: 1.11

This game is best played on a real console — you only need to download a setup program in order to apply the patch to your copy of New Super Mario Bros. No files from the original game are being distributed.

In Newer DS, you'll find...

  • 80 all-new levels on 8 world maps
  • Original and ported graphics and sounds
  • The return of a classic power-up
  • New and updated game mechanics
  • A brand-new soundtrack featuring old and new hits
